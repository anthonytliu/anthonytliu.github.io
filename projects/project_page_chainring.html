FEA-Optimized Chainring

1 Oct 2023

Since getting into track cycling and racing, I wanted to explore how chainring design would affect performance. I aimed to minimize the two main sources of power loss: friction and flexion. My tooth profile is designed to interface perfectly with the roller bearings of the Izumi Super Toughness Chain, the most popular chain for track cycling. The stepped cutouts are the result of FEA analysis optimized for mass-to-stiffness ratio. The chainring is machined from 7075-T6 aluminum with a low friction hard anodize coating.
