CNC & Carbon Fiber Seatpost

16 August 2022

I wanted to create a seatpost that would match the aesthetic of my bicycle fork and decided to make it out of the same materials: aluminum and carbon fiber. The clamping mechanism of the seatpost was designed in Solidworks and prototyped in PLA. The carbon fiber is filament-wound around a mandrel to create a rod which is both laterally stiff and vertically compliant. All the hardware is stainless steel to prevent oxidation with the clamping interface and chemically sealed to prevent any corrosion.
