Titanium Track Bike

7 May 2022

This bike was born out of the desire to own and ride a 1992 Cannondale Track without having to pay the cult-status premium. I did not want to take the risk riding 30+ year old aluminum and designed the geometry with titanium in mind. I raised the bottom bracket and changed the tubing diameters while preserving the integrity of the iconic track bike.
