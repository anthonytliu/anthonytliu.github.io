Titanium Track Bike

7 May 2022

A natural continuation from the carbon fiber front rack project, this titanium front rack extended the modular design and was iterated upon to take full advantage of the material properties. It weighs in at a light 440g/15.5oz and is designed to handle loads up to 15kg/33lbs. The three bolts where the supporting legs meet the body of the rack along with the sliding L-bracket creates a wide range of adjustment.
